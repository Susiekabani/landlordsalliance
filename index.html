Why 1–5 Property Landlords Are Your Best Audience

43% of UK landlords own a single property. 39% own 2–4. LLA members mirror this exactly. Unlike large portfolio operators with corporate accounts and institutional relationships, these landlords actively shop the market — for insurance, mortgage brokers, letting agents and tradespeople — every single year. Real decisions. Real spend. Real reach.
