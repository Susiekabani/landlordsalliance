Why 1–5 Property Landlords Are Your Best Audience

43% of UK landlords own a single property. 39% own 2–4. LLA members mirror this exactly. Unlike large portfolio operators with corporate accounts and institutional relationships, these landlords actively shop the market — for insurance, mortgage brokers, letting agents and tradespeople — every single year. Real decisions. Real spend. Real reach.

Sector UK Market Size Why They Want Your Audience
Landlord Insurance £912m GWP · +7% YoY 34% of landlords underinsured. 75% compare quotes at renewal, 53% switch insurer. These landlords shop the open market every year.
Buy-to-Let Mortgages £20.5bn lent 2024 · +12% YoY Smaller landlords rely on brokers to navigate 200+ lenders. Yields at a 10-year high drive active refinancing — trusted referrals are invaluable.
Financial Advisors £15bn+ market Portfolio landlords need ongoing financial planning — pension strategy, portfolio restructuring, inheritance planning. A high-value, recurring advice relationship.

Legal Services High repeat demand The Renters' Rights Act 2025 creates urgent demand for legal advice on section 21, tenant disputes and compliance — exactly what these landlords face.
Property Maintenance Recurring annual spend Maintenance is non-negotiable regardless of portfolio size. Community-vetted tradespeople win loyal, consistent repeat business.

Minimum Commitment

All packages require a minimum 6-month commitment. This gives both parties time to build a meaningful partnership and measure results properly.

Reviews are conducted quarterly, giving you regular checkpoints to assess performance and discuss any adjustments.

Notice Period

To discontinue after your minimum term, we require 30 days' written notice before your next billing date.

This applies to all monthly packages. Annual packages are non-cancellable mid-term but are not auto-renewed without your confirmation.

All new sponsors benefit from a 14-day cooling-off period. If you change your mind within 14 days of your first payment — even if benefits have already been activated — we'll refund you in full, no questions asked.
